Contractors can reach us for onboarding, time tracking, and payment support.

  • • Onboarding & documentation

  • • Payment schedules
  • • Contract-related questions

Response Time

We typically respond within 24–48 business hours.

Business Hours: Monday – Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (GMT)

Escalations

For urgent issues related to active staffing engagements, please include “URGENT” in your email subject line and provide:

  • Your full name
  • Company name (if applicable)
  • Invoice or contract reference
  • Clear description of the issue

Confidentiality & Data Protection

All support requests are handled confidentially. XcelentHire treats client and contractor data with strict care and uses it solely for service-related purposes.
